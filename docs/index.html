Referrably Privacy Policy

Introduction

Referrably ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • LinkedIn Connections Data: When you upload your LinkedIn connections CSV file, we store information about your professional network, including names, companies, positions, and LinkedIn URLs.
  • Personal Information: We collect and store your first and last name to personalize messages sent through the extension.
  • Browsing Data: We automatically collect information about the job listings you view on supported websites (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor) to identify potential referral opportunities.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To match your LinkedIn connections with companies from job listings you view
  • To generate personalized referral request messages
  • To provide you with relevant referral suggestions
  • To improve the functionality and user experience of our extension

Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not transmit or store your data on any external servers. Your connections data and personal information remain within your browser and are only accessible to the Referrably extension.

Data Retention

Your data remains stored locally until you:

  • Upload a new connections file (which replaces the existing data)
  • Uninstall the extension
  • Clear your browser data

Third-Party Services

Our extension interacts with the following third-party services:

  • LinkedIn.com - for viewing profiles and sending messages
  • Indeed.com - for job listing detection
  • Glassdoor.com - for job listing detection

Please note that these services have their own privacy policies that govern their use of your information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data through the extension's options page
  • Update your personal information at any time
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the extension
  • Upload new connections data to replace existing data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension accordingly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our support channels or by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
